Hack the North 2016: Points and Me


Did your teachers in grade school ever reward you for doing well in class with stars and/or stickers? That's the idea behind Points and Me.

Points and Me allows teachers to register a class. For each class, they can setup achievements and tasks for the students to accomplish, how much each achievement/task is worth in points, redeemable rewards, and how much rewards are worth. Students can login and track the achievements and tasks they have accomplished, how many points they have to spend, and purchase the rewards using their points. Once a student has made a purchase, the teacher is alerted.
